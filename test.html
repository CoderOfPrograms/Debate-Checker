How this website works:

The purpose of this website is to check your debate files for any words that can be considered abelist, racist, and/or feminist. You can also use this website to check out your opponent's case for any troubles. The Checker checks the case for the problems. Simply paste the case in the box and hit submit. You will get a list of words back. If you want the card corresponding to why the word is bad, move on to the library and type in your words. If you want you can just copy paste all your words. The website will register all of them and deliver a card for each one of the words.
